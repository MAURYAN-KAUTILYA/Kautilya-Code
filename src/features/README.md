# Features

This folder groups the routed product experiences.

## Areas

- `auth/` - sign-in and sign-up flows
- `builder-lab/` - the main build, review, and preview workspace
- `dashboard/` - the control surface for sessions and platform entry points
- `introduction/` - the landing story and product narrative

## Why It Matters

Each feature folder is a visible chapter of the product. Together they shape the story that users actually feel when they open the app.
